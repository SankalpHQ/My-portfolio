About Me

Hello! I'm Sankalp, a passionate developer and designer driven by a vision to innovate and make meaningful contributions to the tech industry.

As a MERN Stack Developer and UI/UX designer, I thrive in the space where logic meets creativity. I build full-stack applications using MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js, with expertise in designing RESTful APIs, managing databases, and creating responsive, accessible user interfaces. My design process is rooted in wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing.

Currently pursuing a BCA from Galgotias University, my foundation is reinforced by an Advanced Diploma in Computer Software (DSDC), a NIELIT O-Level certification, and Skill India Mission training. I have a strong command of Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA) using C++. I am also proficient in JavaScript and Python.
